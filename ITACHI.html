POWER AND SKILL

Itachi is considered one of the most powerful and skilled ninjas in the Naruto universe. His mastery of the Sharingan allows him to perceive and copy techniques, anticipate movements, and cast powerful genjutsu (illusionary techniques). He is also proficient in various elemental jutsu and possesses the powerful Amaterasu and Tsukuyomi techniques.


PERSONALITY

Itachi is portrayed as calm, composed, and enigmatic. Despite his stoic exterior, he harbors deep emotions and carries the weight of difficult decisions. His actions are ultimately driven by a desire to protect his younger brother and the Hidden Leaf Village, even if it means being labeled a traitor.


BACKGROUND

Itachi Uchiha was born into the prestigious Uchiha clan, a family known for its powerful ninja abilities and the Sharingan eye technique. From a young age, Itachi displayed exceptional talent and intelligence, quickly rising through the ranks of the ninja world. However, his story is marked by tragedy and difficult choices.
